About us

Welcome To Emergency Fundraiser

The Emergency Fundraiser (CVF) is a Pan India presence independent, non-profit research and advocacy organization working in extending medical support, orphanage support, educational support, rural school support and tribal support for children. CVF gets its core funding from individuals for its various projects. Our prime focus is on being an agent of change for children in India suffering from cancer.
Through our flagship initiative "We Care for You" our organization strives to provide holistic
support to a child suffering from cancer as well as be a pillar of support for the child's family,
right from the point of cancer detection to treatment and the entire healing process.
